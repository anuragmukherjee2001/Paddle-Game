How To Play:-

1) Move your mouse to play the game.

2) You are representing the left player. Defeat the computer to win thw game.

3) Hit the ball every time it comes to you. If you miss the ball, computer will get a point, and if computer misses the ball, you will get a point.

4) The player who reaches 3 points first, will be declared as winner.
